What?

Perhaps a quick word of warning. Do you like the 5-star rating system? This site isn't for you! If, however, you are like me, you've probably spent hours staring at 3-star reviews on Yelp, wondering why some people think the food at any given place is the best while others curse its very existence... How am I supposed to know the truth? Can I handle the truth? Is there truth? And what's an acceptable rating? Are 3 stars enough? 4? Whhhhhhyyyyy? Anyway, you might enjoy this site.

While we are on this subject, let's talk about choices. How many hours have you spent searching for food? Searching for places to eat, arguing about the best places to eat, walking past places and hoping they have good food...? In the immortal words of George Carlin: "too many choices America. It's not healthy." So, guess what? This site takes out the guess work. All you have to do is unequivocally trust me. Why bother with finding a place to eat? I've done it for you. Just eat where I eat.

Oh, and if you were wondering what I mean by "two pinches," I mean the thing Italian grandmothers do. You know, this. With all of that out of the way, allow me to explain these ratings:

  1. 0 Pinches = I wouldn't eat there again. Is it good? Maybe. I don't know.
  2. 1 Pinch = I would eat there again. Most restaurants will fall into this category.
  3. 2 Pinches = I would eat there every day (not really but kind of). The best restaurants I have been to.
